About Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game created by Swedish programmer and game designer Notch. It allows players to build and destroy various types of blocks in a 3D procedurally generated world.

Details about the game

  • It was released in 2011
  • You can play in survival mode or creative mode
  • It is available on multiple platforms
  • It has a large and active community of players and modders
